Self-classifying MNIST Digits

Achieving Distributed Coordination with Neural Cellular Automata

Hello! Here I wanted to demonstrate all great capabilites of my demo! It is based on a well-known distill.pub article and allows you to experiment with three different model varitations. Have a look how differential and learnable rules act to classify each pixel of a drawn digit from 0 to 9.

Available models:

1.Mutating model does its best using mutating pool for regularizion and sample pool not to forget what it learned.
2.Persistent model uses sample pool but does not mutate examples.
3.Naive model just tries to optimize l2 loss between updates.
